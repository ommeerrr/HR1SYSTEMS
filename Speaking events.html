Alexander-Ali Dalipi
Doctoral Student, MSHRD, PHR

CEO & Founder, HR1Systems

Request to Speak

For over 20 years, Alexander-Ali Dalipi has been at the forefront of human resources and payroll, expertly guiding companies through all facets of HR and payroll operations. Known for his strategic vision, Alexander-Ali has masterfully designed and implemented human resource and payroll systems, significantly enhancing departmental efficiency and effectiveness. His leadership and innovative restructuring have consistently driven organizational success, making him a leader in the industry. Alexander-Ali is currently pursuing his Doctorate of Education in Organizational Leadership, Development, and Change (EDOL) at Lehman College and holds a Master's degree in Human Resources Development from Villanova University. Alexander-Ali is renowned for his expertise in HR information systems, payroll, AI, and data analytics. He has been invited to be a keynote speaker at prestigious events such as the Statewide HR and Payroll Conference, DisruptHR, the State Chamber of Commerce, and SHRM conferences and events. In addition to being a keynote speaker, guest lecturer, and panelist, Alexander-Ali is now entering the world of podcasts with "When Worlds Unite: HR & AI." He is currently recognized as a thought leader in HR and payroll as an exclusive member of the Forbes HR Council. Alexander-Ali has also received several awards for his volunteer services with various organizations. His certifications include PHR, SHRM-CP, and National Payroll Compliance, as well as Data Analytics from IBM. Alexander-Ali is the CEO and Founder of HR1Systems, a boutique firm since 2017, providing full client-side implementation and strategic managed services supporting small to midsize companies in HR and payroll operations. HR1Systems is a certified trusted partner. Alexander-Ali successfully developed his reputation and brand as a leading subject matter expert in the HR information systems and payroll industry. As Chief Executive Officer (CEO) of HR1Systems, he has cultivated client and vendor relationships while also developing a reputable portfolio.
